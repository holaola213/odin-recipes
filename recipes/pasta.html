How to make pasta:

Desciption:

Pasta is a versatile and popular food, typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour, water, or eggs. It is formed into various shapes and cooked by boiling or baking.

Types of Pasta:

  • Spaghetti: Long, thin noodles often paired with a wide variety of sauces, from simple tomato to hearty meat sauces.
  • Penne: Tube-shaped pasta with angled ends, which is great for holding sauces inside. It is used in dishes like baked ziti, a casserole-style dish.
  • Lasagne: Wide, flat sheets of pasta used for layering with fillings like cheese, sauces, and meat to create a baked dish.
  • Ravioli: Pockets of pasta filled with ingredients like cheese or meat.
  • Fettuccine: Flat, thick pasta ribbons, often served with rich, creamy sauces.
